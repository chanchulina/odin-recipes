Description

This zucchini lasagna recipe might be low in carbs, but it's high in flavor!

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Seasonings: salt, pepper, fresh basil, fresh oregano and fresh parsley
  • Beef - it can be replace for ground turkey if you'd like
  • Green bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Frozen spinach
  • Tomato products: tomato paste and tomate sauce
  • Wine
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Slice, salt, and drain the zucchini
  2. Make the meat sauce
  3. Make the tomato sauce
  4. Make the ricotta mixture
  5. Assemble the zucchini lasagna according to the detailed instructions in the recipe
  6. Bake according to the recipe
